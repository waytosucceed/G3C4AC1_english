Pronouns


Overview of Grammar topic:

  • Pronouns: Pronouns are words that take the place of nouns to avoid repetition and simplify sentences

  • Personal Pronoun*: Personal pronouns refer to specific people or things and change form based on number, gender, and case.

  • Possessive Pronouns: Possessive pronouns show ownership or possession.

  • Demonstrative Pronouns: Demonstrative pronouns point to specific things and indicate their relative location or distance.

Type of Pronoun Usage Example
Personal Pronouns Refer to specific people or things. I, me, you, we, us, he, him, she, her, they, them, it
Possessive Pronouns Show ownership or possession. mine, yours, his, hers, its, ours, theirs
Demonstrative Pronouns Point to specific things and indicate location. this, that, these, those
